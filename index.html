Leftover VC

Turning Silicon Valley's leftovers into opportunities

The Thesis

We collect and reinvest leftover capital from:

  • Failed startup write-offs
  • Legal settlement recoveries
  • Returned funds from fraud cases

Investment Process

  • Financial Criteria:
    • Individual or joint net worth exceeding $1 million (excluding primary residence)
    • Income exceeding $200,000 individually or $300,000 jointly in the past two years, with reasonable expectation of maintaining in current year
  • Professional Qualifications:
    • Hold in good standing: Series 7, Series 65, or Series 82 licenses
    • Director, executive officer, or general partner of the issuing company
    • "Knowledgeable employee" of a private fund (for fund investments)
    • Family client of a qualifying family office
  • Entity Qualification:
    • Investment entities with over $5 million in assets
    • Entity where all equity owners are accredited investors
    • SEC/State-registered investment advisers or broker-dealers
