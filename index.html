The Thesis

We collect and reinvest leftover capital from:

  • Failed startup write-offs
  • Legal settlement recoveries
  • Returned funds from fraud cases

Investment Process

  • Individual annual income over $200,000 (or $300,000 joint) in each of the last 2 years, with reasonable expectation of maintaining the same level in the current year
  • Individual or joint net worth exceeding $1 million, excluding the value of primary residence
  • Current holder of Series 7, 65, or 82 licenses in good standing
  • Qualified institutional buyer or "knowledgeable employee" as defined by Investment Company Act
